Client Experience

Creative labour is hard. I view the heart of my practice as facilitation of this process: helping groups of people pregnant with something new to make it real - first in their own minds, then on paper and finally out there in the real world. I call it Creative Midwifery. It has taken me from writing digital roadmaps to walking on stilts to creating educational card-games to prototyping haptic interfaces.
